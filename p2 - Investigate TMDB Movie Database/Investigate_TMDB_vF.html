Conclusions (1):¶

From our analysis we can conclude that the most active directors since 1960 have been Woody Allen and Clint Eastwood. However, their movies have not been among the most popular movies. Movies directed by Christopher Nolan and Quentin Tarantino have been among the most popular movies since 1960

Limitations (1):¶

However, there are certain limitations to the inights drawn from our analysis. First, our analysis is limited to the database (which may not be exhaustive to include all movies released) and the insight may or may not apply to a real world scenario. Second, for our analysis we selected directors who were active beyond a pre-decided activity threshold (Minimum 10 movies released since 1960)

# Define a function to rearrange the genres into different columns for easier analysis
def genre_analysis(genre_list):
    for genre in genre_list:
        df_gen1[genre] = df_gen1['genres'].map(lambda x: 1 if genre in x else 0)

# Function is flexible for all genres, particular genres can be input by user as desired
